Welcome to the Emoji Game Arcade!

Made with ❤️, Code, and a lot of 🎮!

This is a fun-filled mini game arcade developed using Node.js, HTML, and MongoDB Atlas.
Our goal was to create a professional, animated website with engaging logic games, all powered by emojis and no images!

Games include: 🧠 Memory, 🪨✂️📄 Rock Paper Scissors, ❓ Quiz, 🔢 Guess the Number, 🎯 Tic Tac Toe, and 🐍 Snake.

Your name is saved securely in the database, and your gaming journey is tracked across beautiful emoji-powered pages.
